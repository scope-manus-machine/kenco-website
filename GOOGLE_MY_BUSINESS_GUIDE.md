This guide will help you create and optimize your Google Business Profile so Kenco Ltd appears in Google Maps and local search results across New Zealand.

## Step 1: Create Your Google Business Profile

### Initial Setup

1. **Go to Google Business Profile**
   - Visit: [https://business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
   - Sign in with your Google account (use a company email if possible)

2. **Click "Manage now"**

3. **Enter Business Name**
   - Business name: **Kenco Ltd**
   - Make sure it matches your legal business name

4. **Choose Business Category**
   - Primary category: **Medical Supply Store**
   - Additional categories you can add:
     - Healthcare Provider
     - Wholesaler
     - Curtain Supplier and Maker

5. **Add Location Type**
   - Choose one:
     - **Service-area business** (if you don't have a physical storefront customers visit)
     - **Physical location** (if you have an office/showroom customers can visit)
   
   For service-area:
   - Hide your address
   - Specify service areas (e.g., "Auckland", "Wellington", "All of New Zealand")
   
   For physical location:
   - Enter your full address
   - Make sure it's accurate for Google Maps

6. **Add Contact Information**
   - Phone: **+64 21 029 66718**
   - Website: **https://kenco.nz**

7. **Verify Your Business**
   - Google will send a verification code via:
     - Postcard (most common - takes 5-7 days)
     - Phone call
     - Email (if eligible)
     - Video verification (for some businesses)
   - Enter the code when it arrives to complete verification

## Step 4: Manage Reviews

### Encourage Customer Reviews

**How to Get Reviews:**
1. After successful installations, send follow-up emails asking for feedback
2. Share your Google review link: 
   - Find it in your GMB dashboard under "Get more reviews"
   - Format: `https://g.page/r/[YOUR_UNIQUE_CODE]/review`
3. Make it easy - include direct link in email signatures
4. Respond to ALL reviews (positive and negative) within 24-48 hours

**Review Response Templates:**

**Positive Review:**
```
Thank you [Name] for your kind words! We're thrilled that our Endurocide curtains/ShadeCare blinds are meeting your infection control needs. We appreciate your trust in Kenco Ltd and look forward to supporting [Facility Name] in the future.
```

**Negative Review:**
```
Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We take all concerns seriously. I'd like to discuss this further and find a solution. Please contact me directly at [email/phone] so we can address this promptly.
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Incomplete profile** - Fill out every section
❌ **No photos** - Add at least 10 high-quality images
❌ **Ignoring reviews** - Respond to all reviews promptly
❌ **Inconsistent NAP** - Name, Address, Phone must match everywhere online
❌ **No posts** - Regular updates signal an active business
❌ **Wrong category** - Choose the most specific category
❌ **Keyword stuffing** - Write naturally for humans, not algorithms
